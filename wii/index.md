## My Thoughts On The Wii

I feel like no matter what I say i'll never show the Wii the recognition it deserves. The Wii played a key part in atleast my childhood and i'd take the bet a lot of other peoples too, So many amazing games such as Zelda: Skyward Sword, the two Mario Galaxy games, and all the Wii name games like Wii Party, Wii play, and Wii Sports. They are just all so amazing to the point I have three Wii's one is a normal pal one, one a modded pal one, and my third is a japanese wii. It is probably in my top three for Nintendo Consoles.
