## My Thoughts On The Wii U

I have a very controversial opinion on the Wii U as I actually really like it, the gamepad connectivity with the console is sometime a bit annoying but I actually think the roster of games on the Wii U is one of the best that Nintendo has there is just so many good games for the time like Mario Kart 8, Mario 3D World, Nintendo Land, Zelda: Wind Waker HD, and Yoshi's Wooly World just to name a few. I always end up coming back to the Wii U for one reason or another.
