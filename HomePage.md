# ForgottenGuides

Welcome to my collection of guides and opinions on the games I play or just like watching.

Here you will find games categorized by console then the game page itself then guides wether it just be 100% guides or retroachievements or something else entirely.

---

## About Me

At the time of writing this I am a 23 year old solo game dev who goes by Forgotten or Josh.
Since I was a kid i always wanted to write guides for games but over the years when other things took a spotlight I forgot about this hobby of mine.
My first guides were really bad and written on physical pages some of which I still have and I thought now while I'm looking for work I may as well try this.

What will be included in this webside is:
- My thoughts on a game on its main page.
- The guides for said game link on it's page
