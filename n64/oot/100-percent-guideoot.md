# The Legend Of Zelda: Ocarina Of Time - 100% Guide

This Guide will cover anything you need to do to 100% the game.
I will go over progression and flag anything if it is missable otherwise the guide will follow this format:
Progression then Collectibles.

## 100% Entails

- Clear All Dungeons And Bosses
- Get All Weapon/Ability Upgrades/Items
- All Heart Pieces
- All Golden Skulltulas
