## About Me

At the time of writing this I am a 23 year old solo game dev and streamer who goes by Forgotten or Josh. Since I was a kid I always wanted to write guides for games but over the years when other things took a spotlight I forgot about wanting to make this a hobby. My first guides when I was younger were really bad and written on physical pages then folded into like a mini booklet of sorts some of which I think I still have. Now while I have some free time I thought I might as well try to make some guides. My style of making them will maybe not be 100% in depth like for example there three tokens in some levels in Crash 1 i'll tell you which level they are in but not exactly where as I think It's more fun so have to figure some stuff out yourself.

This website is one of my first but already way better than any I made in summer classes as a kid, this site will mainly include:

My thoughts on a game on it's main page as well as some generic details about the game such as release time, ratings, and developer.
The guides for games linked on its page whether it be 100% guides or maybe certain challenge run guides,  or even retroachievements guides. Maybe even for pokemon I could make some mini shiny hunting guides only  the future will tell.
