## My Thoughts On The Switch 2

The Switch 2 is so amazing! It is Nintendo's newest console and personally by far it is the best, the games play really smooth as well as having an amazing list of enhanced games the newly released games are really good. Donkey Kong Bananza I first played wearing a full banana costume just because I was so excited for it and I was not disappointed at all! Mario Kart World was really fun, being able to explore more of the karting world in an open world environment was so cool. Pokemon ZA is amazingly fun to play to the point i've replayed it 100% twice and i'm really excited for the eventual Pokemon Winds & Waves.
