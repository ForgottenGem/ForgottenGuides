## My Thoughts On The PlayStation 2

The PS2 was such an amazing console for its time having amazing games such as the birth of the Kingdom Hearts franchise which since then has held a special place in my heart that can never be beat. Kingdom Hearts I remember playing daily when I was a kind on the PS2 to the point my disk shows significant wear but for their former IP's such as Crash Bandicoot or Spyro they were sort of going through a weird phase with Spyro trying to get more edgy with the Legend series of games and Crash having Wrath of Cortex which personally I love. Overall I'd say the PS2 rightfully has a title as one of the greatest retro consoles.
